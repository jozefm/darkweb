Darkweb

Reputation Monitoring, Protection and Investigations

Course Details

Introduction

Introduction to the Darkweb, Darknets and the seedy underworld, as well as common misconceptions, terminology, Debunking myths and next steps.

The Tor Network

Past, Present and Future of the Darkweb and Darknets. The Tor Network; Onion Routing, structure, capabilities, addresses, relays and bridges, and more.

Locating Goods

Locating websites, services and resources. Tor Email, Webservers, Social Networks, Forums, blogs, Chat applications, and more.

Playing it Cool

Learn tactics and techniques to protect your identity while doing research on the Darkweb. Anonymity, Sock Puppets, Persona's. VPN, Socks & Proxies.
